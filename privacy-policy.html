The Look-Out Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The Look-Out ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using The Look-Out mobile application.

Information We Collect

The app may collect the following information:

  • Your device's location (only when permission is granted).
  • Community reports that you voluntarily submit.
  • Optional notes you include with reports.
  • Technical information necessary for the app to function.

Location Information

The Look-Out uses your device location to display nearby incidents, allow reporting based on your current location, and show nearby services such as police stations, hospitals, security companies and other relevant facilities.

Location is only accessed after you grant permission.

Maps

The application uses Google Maps and Google Places services to display maps, search locations and provide navigation assistance.

Firebase

Community reports are securely stored using Google Firebase. Information submitted through reports is used solely to provide community reporting functionality.

How We Use Information

  • Provide community reporting features.
  • Display nearby incidents.
  • Improve app performance.
  • Maintain service reliability.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may only be shared with trusted service providers required to operate the application, such as Google Maps and Firebase.

Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect information stored within the application.

Children's Privacy

The Look-Out is not directed at children under 13 years of age.

Your Choices

You may disable location permission at any time through your device settings. Some features may not function correctly without location access.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.
